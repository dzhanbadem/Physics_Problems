Visualizing why $45^\circ$ is the optimal launch angle for maximum distance.

Theoretical Proof

Range formula:

R = (v₀² sin(2θ)) / g

To maximize $R$, we need $\sin(2\theta) = 1$.

This occurs when $2\theta = 90^\circ$, meaning $\theta = 45^\circ$.
